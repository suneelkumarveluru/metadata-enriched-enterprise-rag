# Literature Review Plan

## Research Theme

Metadata-Enriched and Policy-Aware RAG for AI-Ready Enterprise Data Platforms

## Purpose

This literature review will identify existing work, unresolved problems, and research gaps related to enterprise Retrieval-Augmented Generation, metadata management, data governance, RAG evaluation, and policy-aware access control.

## Key Research Questions

1. How do current RAG systems handle enterprise metadata?
2. What are the limitations of basic vector-search RAG in regulated environments?
3. How is RAG accuracy, faithfulness, and citation quality evaluated?
4. How do enterprise systems enforce access control during retrieval?
5. Can metadata enrichment improve retrieval accuracy and governance compliance?
6. What gaps exist in current enterprise RAG architectures?
